How To Play

Here you'll be tought how to play a basic af game

Drag you finger or cusor to the location your snake shall slithers to

You need to consume all points before they disappear

Once you've gained a point it will be appended to you snake

But beware you cannot consume your self! Otherwise it would be way to simple

Play against AI

To play against an AI press the "ENABLE COMPUTER" button in the home screen

Choose difficulty of AI in home screen and then click play

Everytime you consume a point you gain a life, everytime the AI consumes a point you lose a life
